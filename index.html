SARDAR JODH SINGH ( FOUNDER )

JIS College of Engineering (JISCE) was founded by Sardar Jodh Singh. He is also the chairman of the JIS Group, which oversees multiple educational institutions in India. The college was established in the year 2000 and is located in Kalyani, West Bengal .

JIS College of Engineering (JISCE) is a prestigious private autonomous engineering institution located in Kalyani, West Bengal. Established in the year 2000, it is affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT) and holds accreditation from NAAC with Grade 'A'. The college offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.Tech, M.Tech, BBA, MBA, and MCA, catering to students aspiring for careers in engineering, management, and technology.
